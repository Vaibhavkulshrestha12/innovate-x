About

Innovate X is a 24 hrs hackathon for both newer and experienced coders.

At Innovate X, we believe that innovation thrives in an environment of collaboration, experimentation, and open-mindedness. Our mission is to provide a space where participants can unleash their creativity, push the boundaries of what's possible, and turn their wildest ideas into reality.

• Whether you're a seasoned hacker or a first-time participant, Innovate X offers something for everyone. From coding challenges to design sprints, from IoT to AI, our hackathon covers a wide range of topics and technologies, ensuring that there's always something new to learn and explore.

• But Innovate X is more than just a hackathon; it's a community. It's a place where like-minded individuals come together to share knowledge, exchange ideas, and inspire one another. Through workshops, mentorship sessions, and networking opportunities, participants have the chance to connect with industry experts, build lasting relationships, and take their skills to the next level.

If you're new to Hackathon and want to join, please check out our Code of conduct!
